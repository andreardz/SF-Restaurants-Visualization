SF Restaurants

Desc: Plots list of SF Restaurants on map. Allows user to move two red and blue "spotlights"
of changable radius with a center point of interest to show retaurants that are in their area. 
Highlights restaurants in purple when they are in the intersection of the two spotlights. 
Allows users to filter plotted restaurants by minimum inspection scores.

Additional resources used:

https://bl.ocks.org/mbostock/22994cc97fefaeede0d861e6815a847e // overlap
